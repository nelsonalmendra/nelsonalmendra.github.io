Divide by Box

Divide by Box is a puzzle game that I've made in my spare time and it's available on the Google PlayTM store.

The game has 6 different levels starting in 5x5 (5 rows per 5 columns) until 10x10 which is the highest difficulty. The objective is to divide the grid into rectangular and square pieces such that each piece contains exactly one number, and that number represents the area of the rectangle.

It was built using the Unity3D engine.

Feel free to try it out and leave feedback. Hope you enjoy it.
